About our hotel

The History of our hotel

The HAI hotel was found in 1982 by Haruna Alih Idris. Eight years later(1990), HAI won the award for the best hotel in west Africa in United Kingdom at the WORLD HOTEL AWARD ceremony. We have won multiple awards after that moment, currently we are the best hotel in Africa and rated 3rd in world ranking.
